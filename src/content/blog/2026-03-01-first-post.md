Welcome to my personal site! This is my first blog post where I introduce myself and share what I'm working on.

## About This Site

I built this site using [Astro](https://astro.build), a modern static site generator that's fast, flexible, and developer-friendly. I chose Astro because it:

- Ships zero JavaScript by default
- Supports my favorite frameworks (React, Vue, Svelte)
- Has excellent content collection support
- Makes building fast websites simple

## What I'll Be Writing About

- **Industry observations** - Thoughts on the tech landscape

I'm currently exploring the latest developments in AI and how they apply to web development. The pace of innovation is incredible, and I'm excited to share what I learn along the way.
